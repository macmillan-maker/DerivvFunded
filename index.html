Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I use an EA or bot?
Yes, automated trading is permitted within the defined risk rules. EAs must respect all drawdown and position limits.
When do I get paid?
Funded traders receive payouts on a fixed weekly schedule once payout conditions are met. No surprises, no rule changes.
Is my evaluation fee refunded?
Yes. The challenge fee is refunded with your first payout after successfully passing and receiving a funded account.
What markets can I trade?
Derivv Funded offers Synthetic indices (Boom, Crash, VIX, Step, Jump) and CFD instruments including Forex pairs.
Can I scale my account?
Yes. Consistent performance and adherence to risk limits makes you eligible for capital scaling over time.
What if I fail the evaluation?
Failing doesn't mean you're a bad trader. You can restart any evaluation. Review your performance and come back stronger.
